Frequently Asked Questions

What is an insulated concrete form (ICF) system?

An ICF system is a permanent formwork solution combining reinforced concrete with integrated insulation to create a structurally strong and thermally efficient wall assembly.

What types of projects are suitable for ICF construction?

ICF systems are suitable for residential, commercial, institutional, and industrial buildings, including low-rise and mid-rise applications.

How does ICF improve energy performance?

Continuous insulation and airtight construction reduce heat transfer, leading to lower cooling loads and improved indoor comfort.

Does G-Walls provide technical support?

Yes. G-Walls provides technical documentation and engineering support to assist designers, consultants, and contractors.
